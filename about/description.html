Dustin’s style starts with acoustic pop country music with an alternative sound. His vocal arrangements have been compared to Shawn Mendes, The Killers, Hedley, Coldplay, and David Usher to name a few. Recently Dustin recorded a new EP, produced and recorded in Victoria, British Columbia. Dustin is currently playing local pub shows at some of Vancouver Island’s favorite establishments. Dustin knows how to engage an audience, by giving a great performance and the crowd keeps coming back for more.

Dustin’s musical experience began at the age of 5 when Dustin began singing alongside his mother, Carole Child, a well-respected musician in British Columbia who has played music across the province. His first tour across Canada took place in 2000. Where Dustin successfully played shows at a wide variety of venues shared by some of the most well known bands in Canada. Dustin is available for co-writing, opening acts, fundraisers, and private events. You can find a sample of Dustin’s music here.
